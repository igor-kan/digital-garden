$\chi_{A}:\mathbb{X}\twoheadrightarrow \{ 0,1 \}$ is a [[simple function]]

$$\mathbb{1}_{A}(x)= \chi_{A}(x):= \begin{cases}1 \quad \text{if } x \in A \\ 0 \quad \text{otherwise }    \end{cases}$$
