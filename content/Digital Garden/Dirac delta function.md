$\delta_{x}:\mathcal{P}(\mathbb{X})\rightarrow\{ 0,1 \}$ is a [[set function]]

For $x \in \mathbb{X}, A \subseteq \mathbb{X}$
$$\delta_{x}(A)=\begin{cases} 1 \quad \text{if } x \in A \\ 0 \quad \text{otherwise }\end{cases}$$
